Update the Reviews section on both desktop and mobile.

The current version is not quite right yet.
I want the section to feel more premium, layered, and modern.

1. Desktop version

Use a split layout:

- LEFT side:
  - keep one separate image
  - the image should stay clean and independent

- RIGHT side:
  - create a rotating carousel with 3 visible review cards
  - one main active card in the center/front
  - one smaller card on the left behind it
  - one smaller card on the right behind it

Desktop card behavior:
- center card should be the largest and most visible
- side cards should be smaller, slightly faded, and slightly rotated
- side cards should sit behind the center card
- cards should overlap slightly
- use depth with z-index and scale
- the whole carousel should feel like a real rotating card carousel, not a flat slider

Desktop animation:
- cards should rotate automatically
- movement should be smooth and slow
- center card moves slightly back and sideways
- one of the side cards comes forward into the center
- animation should follow a curved / carousel feeling, not basic horizontal sliding

Desktop layout adjustments:
- move the carousel slightly higher
- make the active card slightly larger than now
- reduce empty space around the carousel
- visually balance it better with the image on the left

2. Mobile version

The mobile version should be different from desktop.

I do NOT want:
- a large image first
- then a separate large review card below it

Instead:

- use the image as the main base
- place a much smaller review card on top of the image
- the card should float over the lower part of the image
- keep it compact and elegant

Mobile card size:
- make the review card much smaller
- reduce padding
- reduce font size slightly
- keep it readable, but compact
- make it feel like a floating mini-card, not a full block

Mobile carousel behavior:
- show one small active card over the image
- optionally show a slight hint of another card behind it
- allow swipe left/right
- auto movement is optional, but if used, keep it slow and smooth

Mobile design goal:
- image remains the main visual
- review card is a small overlay
- whole section should take less vertical space
- keep it modern, clean, and premium

3. Final goal

Desktop:
- photo on the left
- premium rotating layered carousel on the right

Mobile:
- photo as the main element
- smaller review card over the photo
- compact, elegant testimonial composition

This should feel like a premium testimonial section, not a basic slider or a big stacked block.
