# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages
- Service landing pages
- Pricing and guide pages
- Public legal / policy pages that are intended to be indexed

## Out of scope
- Authenticated or internal admin routes (`/admin/**`)
- Transactional / post-payment routes (`/secure-booking`, `/pay/**`, `/payment-success`)

## Target audience
- People in London and nearby areas looking for removals, man-and-van, furniture delivery, waste removal, or international moving services.

## Primary keywords
- London removals
- man and van London
- house moving London
- waste removal London
- rubbish clearance London
- van size guide London move
- removals pricing London

## Notes
- Frontend is a Vite + React + Wouter SPA with a single static `index.html` shell.
- Public routes are currently client-rendered.
